About Sultan's Palace

Welcome, Sultan Palace Restaurant opened in 2019 in Mosul, the first fully-fledged restaurant with a Syrian staff with extensive experience and professionalism in providing Eastern and Western cuisine and services.

At Sultan Palace Restaurant, we strive to provide a unique experience in a calm, family-friendly atmosphere and a diverse menu. We also strive to deliver a high standard of service by skilled craftsmen, applying food safety and quality standards, and at prices that are affordable for everyone.

"We offer a unique, diverse, and rich menu, featuring both Eastern and Western desserts, along with a selection of the finest natural juices and signature cocktails. Additionally, we provide a variety of premium hookahs (arakeel) for an unforgettable experience. To ensure the best customer service, we offer food delivery anywhere within Mosul. We also cater to your family and social events by organizing and arranging the finest food menus and open buffets, designed to suit all tastes in our community and perfectly complement your occasions."

We have unique and successful experiences with international and local organizations and events, both inside and outside the restaurant. We offer the finest menus and services, and provide VIP halls and lounges equipped with the latest audio-visual equipment in a professional manner. Our mission is to exceed your expectations. Sultan Palace - an experience filled with quality and passion...
